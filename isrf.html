Guide for NEW patient

Are you a NEW WALK-IN PATIENT?

  1. Provide your details and the services you came for at any of our designated instant service centers.

  2. After registration, make sure to collect your PID (Patient Identification) Number and proceed to make your payment at nearest payment point.

  3. After Payment, please wait patiently at the waiting room till you are called into the consultation room for procedure.

Guide for REPEAT patient

Are you a FOLLOW-UP (RETURNING) PATIENT?

  1. Provide your PID (Patient Identification) Number and the services you came for at any of our designated instant service centers.

  2. After registration, proceed to make your payment at nearest payment point.

  3. After Payment, please wait patiently at the waiting room till you are called into the consultation room for procedure.
