Our History

The Hospital Management Committee approved the establishment of an Electronic Medical Records (EMR) Unit under the supervision of the Medical Director on the 12 th of July 2022, to strengthen the EMR implementation process and ensure its perfect adoption in the Centre

This unit is headed by Dr O.O Kehinde and supported by 18 other staff drawn from various departments including HIM, ICT, Accounts, Audit, Laboratory and Pharmacy among others. The Unit also has eight sub-unit for effective co-ordination of the unit.

At FMC abeokuta, we believe that collaboration is key to achieving true healthcare transformation. We partner closely with our clients to understand their unique needs and challenges,and we provide ongoing support and training to ensure they maximize the value of our solutions.

We invite you to explore our website, learn more about our innovative EMR solutions, and discover how we can help you transform your practice and deliver exceptional care to your patients.
